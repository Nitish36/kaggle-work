	Bayes Theorem
		P(A|B)=P(B|A)P(A)/P(B)
P(A|B)=Prior Probability
P(A)=Event
P(B)=Hypothesis
P(B|A)=Posterior Probability
	Probability of hypothesis before getting the evidence
to the probability of hypothesis after getting the evidence
